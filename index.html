The Short Break

  • Go for a walk
  • Unplug, rest your eyes
  • Check email
  • Stand up and stretch
  • Organize workspace
  • Prepare the next task

The break is vital to letting your mind recover and refresh after intense focus. At the same time, it triggers a reminder for your body to keep moving.

Find your rhythm

25|5 Classic

The traditional way of getting stuff done. Focus on a task for 25 minutes then take a 5-minute break. Repeat 4 times.

20|10 Moderate

This is a less taxing way to work. Focus for 20 minutes then take a 10-minute break. Repeat 4 times.

45|15 Power

Sometimes you have to power through a lot of work. Focus for 45 minutes the take a 15-minute break. Repeat 3 times.
