SILO |trainer|

Interval training, for your wrist.

Silo watches the clock–so you don't have to.

The Power of Touch and Time

Make your workout more effective with haptic cues between exercise and recovery intervals. Using the Watch Taptic Engine, Silo gives you sharp sense of time and the motivation to get it done.

Set it + Forget It

The simple interface lets you set multiple different interval timers and repetitions. When you’re ready to start, simply raise your wrist and press “GO”.

Stay Ahead of the Game—At your Own Pace

At the end of a workout, Silo commends you and gives you a glance at your heart rate. This helps you know your limit and keep track of your results over time. We're excited about future possibilities and integrating with HealthKit.

HIIT - Tabata - Crossfit

Designed for variety workouts that burn fat and build muscle, Silo lets you set timers across a number of different fitness modes: cardio intervals, strength training, as well as gymnastics movements.

The Most Personal Trainer, Right on your Wrist.

Silo Trainer is a better workout experience for your Apple Watch. A subtle tap on the wrist keeps you on target and lets you finish feeling more productive.
